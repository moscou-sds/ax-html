ABT Me

Im a student from Buenos Aires, Argentina. Who is trying to make an app for help the people that suffer from anxiety.

I know how hard can be for a person that suffer panic attacks, so the best way for make them feel better is by taking with his family circle.

App

The App will have a emergency button that when the person (x) press it immediately calls the contacts entered in the application

Location

At the same time the real-time location of the person (x) is sent to the contacts application.

Audio

Also when the person (x) presses the emergency button, a customizable audio will begin to play, to try to relax and reassure the person.

Call

When a contact answers the emergency signal, a call will be made to be able to speak to the person (x).

Line

The first person whoever responds to the signal will be the one who makes the call, the other contacts will not be allowed to enter the call, but will notify them that a contact is already in communication.
